What the competition is?

There is an Instagram Story competition for Britain SMEs, the theme is "Stayin' Alive". All UK enterprises are welcome to participate, and we are highly welcome to more small businesses and start-up businesses to show your story in Instagram.

The whole competition campaign has four stages. The first stage is a period of registration, start on 1st of March, lasting two months.

The competition has three stages, officially start on 1st of May:

  • The First Round, showing the "Summer Story" of your business.
    From 01.05.2020 to 01.07.2020, Top 25% has qualified into the next round.

  • The Second Round, showing the "Consumer-Centric" story of your business.
    From 01.07.2020 to 01.08.2020, Top 10% has qualified into the final roud.

  • The Final Round, showing the "Environmental Sustainability" of your business.
    From 01.08.2020 to 01.09.2020, there is a winner!
