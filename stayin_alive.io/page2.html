Instagram Story For Business

Instagram Story is a collection of 15-second photos or videos that stay viewable for just 24 hours. it gives you an opportunity to informally promote your business and brands identity, but using it to connect without being too pushy is key.

Why should SMEs use Instagram Story:

    Instagram story stats in the UK:

  • 69% said brands using stories is a great way for people to get to know new products or services.

  • 62% said they have become more interested in a brand or product after seeing it in stories.

  • 31% said they use Instagram Stories to discover a product or brand.

  • 50% of businesses on Instagram create at least one Instagram story in a typical month.

  • 20% of stories posted by businesses result in a direct interaction with users.
